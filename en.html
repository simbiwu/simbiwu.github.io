Security Boundaries

Local by design, with explicit recovery responsibility

SuperWatchDog should be used on devices and files you are authorized to manage. It is not an anti-forensics tool, bypass tool, or offensive security product.

What it does not upload

  • No user files, file lists, vault contents, or local paths are uploaded by the website or core app.
  • Camera-based presence detection is local when enabled; frames are not uploaded by the core design.
  • The website does not embed third-party analytics, ads, or tracking scripts.

What you must keep

  • Keep your BitLocker password and recovery materials yourself.
  • If you forget the password and lose recovery materials, vault contents may be unrecoverable.
  • SuperWatchDog does not save plaintext BitLocker passwords and cannot decrypt a vault for you.
